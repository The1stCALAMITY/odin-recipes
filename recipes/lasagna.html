Lasagna Recipe

This beautiful lasagna dish will make your mouth water with all of the tomato sauce, cheese, savory garlic and crispy cheese coating on top

Ingredients

  • Sheet pasta
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Garlic clove
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef

  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees farenheitt
  2. 1. Boil sheet pasta until al dente
  3. 2. cook ground beef until brown and drain out about half of the greese
  4. 3. pour thin layer of sauce into baking pan
  5. 4. place desired thickness of cheese over sauce
  6. 5. Lay 1 layer of sheet pasta
  7. 6. Repeat steps 3 - 5 until you have no more sauce, pasta, or cheese
  8. 7 place baking pan in oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and light-golden brown
