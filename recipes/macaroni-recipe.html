Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

This mouth watering macaroni and cheese recipe will be life changing for you, your family, and your children

Ingredients

  • desired kind of pasta
  • garlic powder
  • mustard powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cream cheese
  • sharp cheddar cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • milk
  • all purpose flour
  • butter

Steps

  1. 1. start pasta cooking in boiling water for package directed amount of time
  2. 2. now in a separate pot, start melting butter on low-medium heat
  3. 3. once butter is melted, add 3 tbsp of all purpose flour, and mix into butter until you get a smooth paste of flour and butter
  4. 4. pour milk into butter and flour paste and whisk to thicken
  5. 5. while thickening the white sauce, add your seasonings into the mixture
  6. 6. once mixture has thickened enough, remove from heat and pour the shredded cheeses into the mixture and begin stirring with wooden spoon for easier cleanup
  7. 7. after shredded cheeses are melted into the sauce, add cream cheese to the mixture to return it back to the creamy texture
  8. 8. once pasta is finished cooking, strain the water and leave a small amount of water in the pot with the pasta
  9. 9. then proceed to pour the cheese sauce into the pasta and mix until you have fully coated the pasta
